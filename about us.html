about us

treat haven

We are Treat Haven, your local haven for delectable cakes, cupcakes, donuts, brownies, and a variety of other sweets. Nestled in the heart of downtown Los Angeles, we pride ourselves on crafting treats that bring joy to every occasion. Founded by passionate bakers who wanted to share their love for sweet creations, our bakery has been delighting taste buds since 2015. Visit us at 631 S Rivercrest Ave, open Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 6 PM. We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments. Come indulge in our fresh, handcrafted goodies and experience the sweetness of life!
