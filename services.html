services

Business hours

Our bakery is open daily from Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 6 PM. Whether you’re grabbing a morning coffee and croissant or picking up a custom cake for a special occasion, we’re here to meet your needs. For holidays or special events, please check our website for any changes to our regular hours.

Ordering Online

To order items online for pick-up, start by logging into your account on our website or app. Search for the products you want and select the option for in-store or curbside pick-up. Choose your preferred location and time slot, if available. Add items to your cart, review your selections, and proceed to checkout. Enter your contact information and payment details, then confirm your order. You’ll receive a confirmation with pick-up instructions and can collect your items at the chosen time.

Custom Designs

To pick different designs please log into your account first. Then navigate to the "Customization" or "Design Your Treats" section. Browse through available design categories or use filters to refine your choices. Select a style, and then customize details like colors, themes, and decorations using the interactive tools provided. Review your design options, and if needed, upload any custom images or text. Once satisfied, add your creation to the cart, choose pick-up and proceed to checkout.

events & catering

We do offer catering services to our customers! Start by visiting our website and logging into you account. Once logged in try navigating to the "Catering" section. Browse through the menu options, including cakes, pastries, and specialty items. Select the desired items and specify quantities, then choose a delivery date and time. Fill out any customization requests and provide event details. Complete the order by entering your contact and payment information. We will confirm your order and ensure everything is prepared and delivered to meet your event needs.

The Treat Haven App is here!

Would you like to have access to delicious treats at the tip of your fingers? We have an app now! You can download and install it from your app store. Open the app and create an account or log in. Browse through the menu, featuring cakes, pastries, and other treats, and select items you wish to order. Customize your selections as needed and choose a delivery or pick-up option. Review your cart, enter payment details, and confirm your order. The app will provide updates on your order status and we will be sure to keep your treats fresh until you get arrive.
